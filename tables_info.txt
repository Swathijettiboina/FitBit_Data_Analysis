Dimension Tables:
dim_users: Dimension for users.

USER_ID (Primary Key)

USER_NAME (Optional)

USER_EMAIL (Optional)

dim_calendar: Dimension for time-related data.

DATE_ID (Primary Key, could be a combination of ACTIVITY_DATE for daily granularity)

DATE (The date itself)

DAY_OF_WEEK (Day of week, e.g., Monday)

MONTH (Month)

QUARTER (Quarter of the year)

YEAR (Year)

dim_activity_level: Dimension for activity levels (categorical data).

ACTIVITY_LEVEL_ID (Primary Key)

ACTIVITY_LEVEL_NAME (Lightly Active, Fairly Active, Very Active, etc.)

dim_health_status: Dimension for health status from the weight metrics table.

HEALTH_STATUS_ID (Primary Key)

HEALTH_STATUS_NAME (Healthy, Unhealthy, etc.)

dim_intensity: Dimension for intensity categories.

INTENSITY_ID (Primary Key)

INTENSITY_LEVEL (Low, Moderate, High, etc.)

dim_workout_intensity: Dimension for workout intensity.

WORKOUT_INTENSITY_ID (Primary Key)

WORKOUT_INTENSITY_NAME (Light, Moderate, Intense, etc.)
